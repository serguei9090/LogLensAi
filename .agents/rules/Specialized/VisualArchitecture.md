# Visual Architecture Standard

Use diagrams when they reduce ambiguity faster than prose.

## Rules

- Verify nodes and edges before drawing.
- Label every connection.
- Use subgraphs or grouping for systems with more than five nodes.
- Keep one viewpoint per diagram: context, container, component, sequence, or data.
- Store durable diagrams in `docs/architecture/diagrams.md` or the active spec.

## Skill Use

Use `diagram-creator` for complex Mermaid diagrams or when the diagram itself is a deliverable.
